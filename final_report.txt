Time Series Stock Forecasting Report
=====================================

Models evaluated: ARIMA, SARIMA, Prophet, LSTM

RMSE Results:
 - ARIMA: 69.2944
 - SARIMA: 55.1133
 - Prophet: 38.3315
 - LSTM: 5.9821

Key Findings:
1. ARIMA performed the worst with RMSE ~69.
2. SARIMA improved results slightly (RMSE ~55).
3. Prophet captured trend/seasonality well (RMSE ~38).
4. LSTM achieved the best performance with RMSE ~6, showing the power of deep learning.

Conclusion: LSTM outperformed traditional models for this dataset.
